The Challenge

ACME Corp was growing fast but hitting a wall. Their sales team was spending 60% of their time on admin work—data entry, CRM updates, searching for account information, and piecing together insights from scattered sources. Reps were using 7 different tools and manually logging deals into Salesforce.

Lead quality was declining because the team didn't have time to properly research and prioritize prospects. The sales forecast was unreliable, and the VP couldn't confidently tell the board when deals would close. They were losing deals to better-organized competitors who had visibility into their pipelines.

ACME needed a way to reduce busywork, improve lead quality, and get reliable visibility into their pipeline—without hiring more people.

The Problem

The Solution

ACME implemented Kombo across their 40-person sales team. They started with three core features:

  • AI-powered lead ranking: Kombo analyzed their historical closed deals and identified the signals that predicted wins. Reps now prioritize leads automatically scored by buying intent.
  • Automated deal tracking: All calls, emails, and activity auto-logged to Salesforce. No manual updates. Reps just work.
  • Revenue forecasting: Kombo predicted close dates and probabilities with 92% accuracy, giving the VP reliable visibility.

The implementation took 2 weeks. By week 3, the team was fully operational.

The Impact on the Team

For sales reps: They're spending 60% less time on admin work and 60% more time selling. Their pipeline is automatically researched and prioritized. They close deals 24% faster because they're focused on hot prospects instead of busywork.

For sales leadership: They have 92%-accurate visibility into pipeline health and close dates. They can confidently forecast revenue. They understand which reps are on track and where to coach. They've confidently hired 3 new reps this year, knowing the platform will help them ramp fast.

For the company: The 47% revenue growth speaks for itself. But beyond that, the team is happier. Turnover decreased 18%. Sales culture shifted from blame-focused forecasting to data-driven planning.

Key Takeaways

1. AI scales your best practices. Kombo learned what ACME's best reps do and applied it to the whole team. Lead scoring was based on their DNA, not generic rules.

2. Fixing the process is faster than hiring. ACME got 50 new "reps" worth of productivity without the hiring, onboarding, and management overhead. The platform paid for itself in 3 months.

3. Data quality drives growth. When your CRM is automatically updated with real activity, you stop guessing about pipeline health. Better data led to better decisions and 47% growth.

4. Reps will adopt tools that help them sell. ACME didn't have to force adoption. Reps use Kombo because it makes them look smart and closes more deals. Engagement stayed above 95%.
