FAQ

Common questions about KomboAI.

What is KomboAI?
KomboAI is an AI layer that sits on top of your CRM and automates the repetitive work between your tools — scoring leads, drafting outreach, logging activity, and forecasting pipeline.
Can I use KomboAI for free?
Yes. Your first 100 scored leads are free. No credit card required to start. Most teams stay on the free tier for the first month while getting set up.
How does pricing work?
After your free leads, KomboAI charges $0.10 per scored lead. No seat fees, no platform fees. You only pay for what you use.
Does it work with my CRM?
KomboAI works with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and any CRM via REST API. Most setups take under 10 minutes.
Can it be used for outbound?
Absolutely. KomboAI powers some of the highest-performing outbound teams in B2B SaaS. Build prospecting lists, draft first touches, and warm leads on autopilot.
How do I get support?
Email hello@komboai.com or open a chat on any page. Real humans answer within an hour during business hours.
