// What we believe

Five principles
that keep us honest.

We re-read these out loud every Tuesday at standup. They're not posters in the office — they're the questions we ask before shipping anything. Where they conflict, we ship the simpler thing.
// 01

Reps before revenue.

If a feature makes the metric go up but the rep's day worse, we don't ship it. The job is to make selling feel good again.

"Would Marco, the AE, send this to a friend?"
// 02

Agents do the work.

Chatbots describe. Agents act. We build software that takes responsibility for outcomes — and writes back to the systems of record.

"Was this an action, or a suggestion?"
// 03

Trust is earned in audit logs.

Every agent decision is observable, reversible, and explainable. SOC 2 Type II is a floor, not a ceiling. The Trust Center is public.

"Could the customer sue us and lose?"
// 04

Ship every Thursday.

Small batches. Loud changelog. We've shipped 146 weekly releases since v0.1. Slow software is a tax on customers — and on the team.

"What did we ship this week?"
// 05

Remote, with two hubs.

You can work from anywhere. We meet in Madrid & Berlin every quarter. People who want a desk get a desk; people who don't, don't.

"Are we hiring for talent or for time-zone?"
// 06

One honest contract.

If the agent doesn't perform inside 30 days, we refund and unplug — no claw-backs, no lawyers, no shame. We've done it 9 times.

"Is this fair if you read it out loud?"
