Before you send a message…

The 5 questions we get most. Sometimes a 30-second answer beats a callback.

How long does the demo really take?
30 minutes on the dot. We connect to a sandbox version of your CRM at the start, ask 4 questions about your motion, then show the four agents running on real data. No slides. No "next steps deck."
Can I get a custom DPA / MSA / SOC 2 report before our call?
Yes. Everything is on our Trust Center — no NDA, no form. SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, sub-processor list, and a sample DPA are all linked from there. For custom MSAs, ping legal@getkombo.ai.
I'm an existing customer with an urgent issue. How do I escalate?
Open the in-product widget (bottom-right) and use the "Urgent" tag — that pages our on-call. Or email urgent@getkombo.ai with your workspace ID. Enterprise customers have a dedicated CSM Slack channel.
What's your refund / try-then-buy policy?
14-day free trial on Starter and Growth, no card required. After paid onboarding, if the agents don't perform inside 30 days, we refund and unplug — no claw-backs, no NDA-walls. We've done it 9 times in 3 years.
I'm just researching for a future project — should I still reach out?
Yes. We don't run drip nurture sequences (we'd be embarrassed). We'll send 1 email confirming receipt, then check in 90 days from now if you'd like. That's it. No "just bumping this thread" emails. Promise.
