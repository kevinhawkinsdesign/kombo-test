STEP 02 — SEQUENCE

Drafts the first touch, launches it the moment a rep approves.

The sequencing agent writes a personalised first email + LinkedIn note + call talk-track for every priority account. Reps review, edit, and approve in one keystroke. Replies route to the right rep automatically.

  • Personalised on real triggers (funding, hires, role)
  • Multichannel — email, LI, voicemail, SMS
  • Approve in batch (25 sequences in < 3 min)
  • Auto-pauses if a buying signal appears

STEP 03 — COACH

Listens to every call, spots the moments managers miss.

The coaching agent reviews every recorded call against your top reps' patterns. It flags discovery gaps, stalled deals, and competitor mentions before they cost you the quarter.

  • Reads transcripts in 10+ languages
  • Compares to your champion-rep playbook
  • Drops a 90-second weekly digest in Slack
  • Generates one-tap "next-best action" cards

STEP 04 — FORECAST

Tells you which deals will land — while you can still save them.

The forecasting agent fuses CRM stage, call sentiment, response cadence, and historic close-rate. It re-scores every deal nightly and DMs your CRO when confidence drops > 15 points week-over-week.

  • Replaces "guess + pad" pipeline reviews
  • Flags deals stalling 5+ days before stand-up
  • Predicts close date within ±6 days (P50)
  • Plain-English reason for every score

Specialised LLM router
