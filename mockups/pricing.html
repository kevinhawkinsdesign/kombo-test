Pricing questions, answered.

Still have one? We'll answer in 2 hours, by a human, before you ever see a contract.

What counts as an "active rep"?
An active rep is anyone who logs into KomboAI in a calendar month. Sales managers, SDRs, AEs, and CS reps all count the same. Inactive seats are free — pause and resume anytime.
What's an "AI action" and why are they limited on Starter?
An AI action = one agent task: scoring a lead, drafting an email, summarising a call, enriching a contact. Starter caps at 1,000 / rep / month so you can predict spend. Growth and Enterprise are unlimited.
Do you offer discounts for startups, non-profits, or YC companies?
Yes — 50% off Growth for the first 12 months for pre-Series-B startups, YC companies, and registered non-profits. Apply via your account manager.
Can I switch plans mid-cycle?
Yes. Upgrade prorated immediately. Downgrade applies at the next billing cycle. No exit fees, no claw-backs on annual discounts.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You own everything. Export pipelines, sequences, recordings, and audit logs as CSV/JSON anytime. We delete your data within 30 days of cancellation, certified.
How does KomboAI handle GDPR + EU residency?
We're SOC 2 Type II + GDPR-compliant, with EU data residency on Growth and above. Enterprise customers can pick from 6 regions or self-host in their own VPC. See live audit reports, sub-processors, and pen-test summaries on our Trust Center →
Can I see a live demo before paying?
Yes — book a 30-minute demo and we'll show you on your real CRM. No slides. Book a demo →
