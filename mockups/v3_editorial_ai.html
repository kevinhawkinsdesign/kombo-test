The right name, first.

Reps stop guessing. They open the dashboard and dial the right name, in the right order, every time.

CH. 02 — Pipeline Intelligence

The forecast, without the guesswork.

A FIELD REPORT · FROM THE FORECAST DESK

The Monday pipeline review is, for many revenue leaders, a quiet form of theater — a meeting in which everyone agrees the number is plausible while privately suspecting it is not. KomboAI replaces theater with arithmetic. The system reads every deal in the pipe, weighs activity against historical close patterns, and returns a forecast accurate within 94%.

Stale deals, the silent killers of every quarter, are surfaced before they cost you the renewal. The veteran's intuition, in software.

CH. 03 — Automated Outreach

The cold email, warmed.

A LETTER · ADDRESSED TO SARAH CHEN

It is by now something of a cliché to call email dead. The truth is more interesting: email at scale is dead. Email written one-to-one — informed by a buyer's role, signals, and recent moves — has never been more effective. KomboAI drafts those one-to-one emails at the speed of a templated blast.

A single rep can send three hundred personalized openers a week, every one of them tuned to the prospect's world.
