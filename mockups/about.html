We build the agents

KomboAI exists because the modern revenue stack has 47 tools, 12 dashboards, and 0 leverage. We're rebuilding the sales motion as one workflow — agent-first, CRM-native, human-coached.

Software for sales hasn't moved in 15 years. We think that's the point.

Reps spend 60% of their day selling 60% of their day fighting their CRM. The tools they need don't ship: they get bought.

So enterprise sales got more dashboards. More notification noise. More "single panes of glass" stacked on top of more single panes of glass.

We started KomboAI with a simple bet: if AI can read your CRM, your calendar, your inbox, your calls — it can do the work, not just describe it.

Four agents. One workflow. The tab your reps already have open.

// The story so far

From a co-working space in Barcelona
to teams across Europe and beyond.

Three years of building in the trenches — rewrites, real customers, and a product that keeps getting sharper. Here's the timeline as we lived it.

Mar 2023
Two friends, one Notion doc

Ignacio Gaminde and Ale Pintaudi write the first KomboAI manifesto in a Barcelona co-working space. The bet: AI can do the work, not just describe it.

Oct 2023
First customers onboard launch

The product works. Design partners from Shakers, Edicom, and Fever start using KomboAI daily. We hire our first engineers.

Feb 2024
Lead Scoring ships

The first agent does exactly what we promised. Reps wake up to a ranked queue every morning. Reply rates jump on day one.

Jul 2024
100+ customers

Word spreads across Barcelona's startup scene. Teams at Fever, Weekendesk, and Lucca bring KomboAI into their daily motion.

Jan 2025
Sequencing agent ships

Approve-in-Batch hits production. Reply rates on customer accounts jump 2.1×. We learn the shape of compounding leverage.

Sep 2025
GDPR compliant

We close our first enterprise deals. Full compliance across EU data regulations. The Trust Center goes live.

May 2026
Forecasting agent — GA today

The 4th agent ships. The product is now four specialists working as one team. Still in Barcelona. Still obsessed with making reps' lives better.
