About

We believe sales
should be smarter.

KomboAI was born from a simple frustration: sales teams waste too much time on the wrong leads. We're building AI that fixes that — so reps can focus on what they do best: selling.

Ale Pintaudi & Ignacio Gaminde · Co-founders · Barcelona

Mission

Give every SDR and AE the intelligence of a 20-year veteran and the efficiency of an AI-powered machine — without replacing the human element that closes deals.

Vision

A world where no SDR or AE wastes time on a deal that won't close — where AI surfaces the right lead, at the right time, with the right approach.
