Frequently asked questions

What is KomboAI?
KomboAI is a revenue automation layer that sits on top of your CRM. It scores leads, drafts outreach, logs activity, and forecasts pipeline — without your reps changing how they work.
Can I use KomboAI for free?
Yes. Your first 100 scored leads are free, and there's no credit card required to start. Most teams stay on the free tier for the first month while they get set up.
How is the cost calculated?
After your free leads, KomboAI charges $0.10 per scored lead. There are no seat fees, no platform fees, and no surprise bills. You only pay for what you actually use.
Does it work with my CRM?
If you're on Salesforce or HubSpot, you can be live in under 10 minutes. We also offer a REST API and Zapier integration for everything else.
Can it be used for outbound?
Absolutely. KomboAI is the engine behind some of the highest-performing outbound teams in B2B SaaS. Build target lists, draft first touches, and warm leads on autopilot.
How can I contact you?
Email hello@komboai.com or open a chat from any page. Real humans answer within an hour during business hours.
